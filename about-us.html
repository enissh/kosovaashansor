About us

Ferizaj, Kosovo

Kosova - Ashansor - Group SH.P.K.

Ultra Comfort Extra Confidence!

Since 1980, Kosova Ashansor has been carrying out elevator design, production and marketing activities and brings together the products it manufactures with its superior quality approach with 63 countries of the world. It takes its place among the respected companies in the elevator sector with its production facility with 27,000 m² closed area and 23,000 m² open area, high-tech machine park, 210-person expert staff, production that it carries out without compromising on quality and references including world giant companies. We are the first choice in the magnificent projects of our solution partners with our product range covering elevator cabins, elevator doors, elevator buttons, package elevators and all elevator accessories. With our R&D unit, which always works in integration with our production unit, we meet the needs of our customers, who are solution partners, that go beyond the standards, with our innovative designs. Thanks to this flexibility we provide, the products we shape together with technology are an indication that we are one step closer to the future. We continue our successful operations for the respected companies of countries such as Germany, England, Russia, Azerbaijan, Sweden, Georgia, Iraq, Tunisia and UAE, who have chosen us as their solution partner, and we continue to grow and take firm steps on our way with our honesty, quality policy and continuous development approach, which we have valued since the day we were founded.


Our Mission

As Kosova Ashansor, our mission is to provide our solution partners with ultra comfort and extra confidence by following and developing the latest technology in the products we produce without compromising on quality for the sake of low cost.


Our Vision

As Kosova Ashansor Company, our targeted vision is to increase our production capacity and R&D investments, to shape the future by developing the products we design using new technologies, to increase the number of countries we can reach in the globalizing world by raising the “Yükseliş” brand in a way worthy of its name.
